Frequently asked

01 How do I add an item to my list?
Tap the add an item… field at the bottom of any list and type what you need. Mochi will sort it into the right category for you.
02 Are nutrition estimates accurate?
They're approximations based on standard ingredient data and average serving sizes. Treat them as rough guides, not medical or dietary advice.
03 How do I delete a list or an item?
Tap the trash icon in the top right of a list to remove the whole list. Swipe an individual item to remove just that one.
04 I found a bug or have a feature request
Please email support.mochi@gmail.com with as much detail as you can — what you were doing, what happened, and your iPhone model if relevant. Screenshots help a lot.
